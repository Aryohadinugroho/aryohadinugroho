About Devasana Nirmata Isvara

The name of this fashion show activity is DEVASANA NIRMATA ISVARA. The word DEVASANA is Sanskrit, namely deva which means god and bhusana which means clothing. The meaning of the word DEVASANA in this activity is the God of Clothing, which describes the works displayed in the fashion show as having very good quality and creativity or being equated with a god. While NIRMATA ISVARA. In Sanskrit, this name describes the power to create and share light, both literally and symbolically. The creator of light guides enlightenment through creative inspiration and dreams. The moon reflects light in the darkness, while dreams reveal insight. This symbolizes the ability to transform darkness into light and achieve recognition in the fashion industry.
